## VS Code codicons

The reset and persist buttons embed three icon path-data strings —
`discard`, `save`, and `save-as` — extracted unmodified from the
`src/icons/*.svg` files of the codicons project. Paths are inlined
in `src/viewer.js` (look for the `CODICON_PATHS` constant) rather
than shipped as a font.
